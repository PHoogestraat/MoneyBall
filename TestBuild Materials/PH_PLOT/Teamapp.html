Investigating Cost Per Win

The data set included with this exercise depicts time vs cost/win in the NBL by choosing a team located in the upper left portion of the screen. Each datapoint corresponds to the number of wins for a NBL team with additional information after clicking on the datapoint. Data includes: Team name, Championship status, Cost per win, Team salary, Ave player salary, and the year which the data was extracted. Green indicates world champion.
