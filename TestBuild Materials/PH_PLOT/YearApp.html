Investigating Cost Per Win

The data set included with this exercise depicts relationships between wins and cost in the NBL by year. Select the year of interest located in the upper left. Each datapoint represents a NBL team with additional information after clicking on the team. Where the horizontal and vertical red lines intersect represent the average values for cost per win and wins. The lower right quadrant represents teams who have minimized cost and maximized wins. The upper left quadrant represents teams that have paid the most amount of money per win. The areas of interest include: Championship status, Cost per win, Team salary, Ave player salary, and number of wins. Green indicates world champion.
